Tsavo

Tsavo west and west together form one of the world's largest national parks, covering 4% of kenya.The huge,semi arid wildernessnd is home to most safari animals, and although wildlife densities aren't large,spotting animals is always here due to the lack of foliage.The park is a stronghold for elephants, and you'll commonly find them with a reddish apperance find them with a reddish apperance from the distinctive red soil of the area.
